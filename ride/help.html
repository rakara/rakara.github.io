Frequently Asked Questions
How do I request a ride? ‚ñº
Enter your destination on the home screen, select your preferred ride type, and tap "Request Ride". You'll be matched with a nearby driver.
What payment methods are accepted? ‚ñº
We accept M-PESA, credit/debit cards, and TickRide Wallet. You can manage your payment methods in the Payment tab.
How do I cancel a trip? ‚ñº
You can cancel before the driver arrives through the active trip screen. Note that cancellation fees may apply after 2 minutes.
How is the fare calculated? ‚ñº
Fares are based on distance, time, and demand. You'll see the estimated fare before requesting. Surge pricing may apply during peak hours.
What if I left something in the car? ‚ñº
Go to your trip history, find the relevant trip, and tap "Report Issue". Select "Lost Item" and we'll help you connect with your driver.
How do I add a promo code? ‚ñº
On the trip confirmation screen, tap the promo code field and enter your code. The discount will be applied automatically.
