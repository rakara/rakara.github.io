  • Background check clearance

Vehicle

Your vehicle must meet these standards:

  • 4 doors, 5 seats minimum

  • PSV licence (Public Service Vehicle)

Getting Started

1

Download & Install

Download the TickRide Driver app from the link above. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your phone settings if prompted.

2

Sign Up

Create your driver account with your phone number, email, and basic information. This takes less than 5 minutes.

3

Upload Documents

Submit photos of your driver's licence, vehicle documents, insurance, and PSV badge. Our team will review within 24 hours.

4

Get Approved

Once approved, you'll receive a notification. Complete a short orientation video and you're ready to start driving!

5

Start Earning

Go online in the app and start accepting trip requests. Track your earnings in real-time and get paid weekly via M-PESA.
