Here's your Titan-themed redesign of rakara.github.io. Here's what drives the concept:
The Aesthetic Direction — "Primordial Forge"
Titans as primordial gods of craft and raw power informed every decision: obsidian and charcoal stone backgrounds, molten gold as the signature accent (representing forged craftsmanship), ember-red for energy markers, and ancient Roman numeral service icons (I–VIII) echoing stone tablets.
Typography
Cinzel Decorative for the display logo and major headings — it's rooted in Roman inscriptional lettering, the literal language carved by Titans. Cinzel for UI labels and navigation. Cormorant Garamond in light/italic for body copy — classical, editorial, timeless.
Key Design Decisions

Hero title uses the chamfered clip-path CTA button — angular, forged-metal feel, not generic rounded corners
Services grid uses Roman numerals (I–VIII) as section glyphs — deliberate, not decorative
Work cards have giant ghost numerals in the background for depth
The gold top-border slide-in on service card hover feels like gilding stone
Scroll reveal animations bring each section to life progressively
