About the Book

The Kenya edition adapts the global B2B Brand Management framework to the realities of Kenyan industry, policy, and entrepreneurship. It combines academic rigor with hands-on case studies, practical toolkits, and curricula for university adoption.

Key themes include Performance Branding, Transformative Marketing, Digital Transformation, and AI-driven B2B strategy tailored to Kenya's sectors.
