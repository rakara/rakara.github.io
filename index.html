About Me

Building the web, one thoughtful pixel at a time.

HTML5 CSS3 PHP SQL Angular React Vue Next Brand Identity UX Design Social Media Responsive Design

I’m Rakara Abila — a freelance web designer and developer based in Kenya, driven by a genuine passion for creating meaningful digital experiences. To me, a great website goes beyond aesthetics — it’s a strategic asset that communicates your brand, builds trust, and delivers measurable results.

I’ve had the opportunity to work with startups, non-profits, and established businesses across a range of industries. Every project begins with a clear understanding of what matters most — your goals, your audience, and your story. These insights guide every design and development decision.

I stay up to date with evolving web standards and technologies to ensure your website is not only modern, but also scalable and future-ready. My commitment doesn’t end at launch — I provide ongoing support, maintenance, and optimization to help your platform grow alongside your business.

Whether you’re starting from the ground up or looking to elevate your existing presence, I’m here to help you build something purposeful, effective, and truly exceptional.
